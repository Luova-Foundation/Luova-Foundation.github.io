About Us

Those with great passion can take over all that is considered impossible and these enthusiasts who want to make the extraordinary difference is the fuel of our organization. So here we introduce LUOVA (a Finnish word; meaning creative), a community by women tech enthusiasts and students to lift the spirits hidden in young women in the designing sector by providing the requisite knowledge thereby invoking the fire in them

An organization by women tech enthusiasts and students to lift the spirits hidden in young women in the designing sector by providing the requisite knowledge thereby invoking the fire in them.


Our vision is to bring forward women, break their chains of exclusion, educate them and to create colossal opportunities for them. Being together, we aim to meet the anticipation of our members by providing quality education while adhering to the core values. Here, we set up a focused platform paving the way for peer-to-peer or reciprocal learning. Our mission includes accelerating innovation and bringing in a major revolution to the society.

Creating your own websites and blogs

A web designer is someone who is both creative and technically inclined and uses both these qualities to design a website. Pursuing web designing will help you to create a blog template,a simple page or a complete website. You will get to think in HTML, Learn CSS, and study about basic designing tools.

Creates visuals, images and other visually-appealing elements, usually for conveying an idea.

A graphic designer is someone with both artistic and technical skills who assembles together images,typography and motion graphics to create a piece of design.The most common use of learning this is that you can contribute to game development. It can be used for creating marketing products. Pursuing graphic designing helps to acquire technical skills to use design software programs such as Photoshop or Quark Xpress.

Creating 3 dimensional view of objects..

In 3D computer graphics, 3D modelling is the process of giving a mathematical representation of objects in 3 dimensions with the help of a specialized software. It is highly useful to someone wanting to pursue game development, architecture,industrial design etc.
